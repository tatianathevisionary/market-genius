Risk Appetite 101

THE SUPPLY & DEMAND OF COURAGE
Remember econ: price sits where SUPPLY meets DEMAND. Crypto's supply is nearly fixed short-term — so price moves are almost pure demand shifts. "Risk appetite" is WHAT moves the demand curve: how much risk people are willing to hold for a shot at gains. It moves along a ladder: CASH → BONDS → STOCKS → BTC → ETH → SOL/LINK → memecoins safest ◀──────────── the risk ladder ────────────▶ riskiest GREED = demand reaches OUT the ladder → riskiest assets rise FASTEST FEAR = demand retreats DOWN the ladder → riskiest assets fall HARDEST That's the whole concept. Everything below just measures where on the ladder demand currently sits — and it usually turns at the ends BEFORE it turns in BTC.
WHY IT'S TRADEABLE: appetite shifts are gradual and sequenced — memecoins die first, then SOL/LINK fade, then ETH, then BTC. Watching the riskier rungs gives you an early-warning system for the safer ones.

BTC vs Gold · 1Y

THE HEDGE TEST · REBASED %
HOW TO READ: gold is the incumbent safe-haven — the bottom rung of the risk ladder, where money goes when it's scared but still wants a hedge. If BTC were "digital gold," these lines would move together in a crisis. When gold rises while BTC falls (now), the market is voting that BTC is a risk asset, not a hedge — expect it to trade with the Nasdaq, not against it. If BTC ever starts rising with gold during stress, that regime change would be one of the most bullish structural signals possible.

Field Manual

DECISION RULES
  • Durable bottom: needs A ≥ 3 AND B ≥ 3. Either alone is incomplete.
  • Capitulation-reversal entry (aggressive): B1 + B6 in the same ~3 sessions, smallest size, hard invalidation below the capitulation wick low.
  • Basing-breakout entry (conservative): ≥2 weeks flat, then range-high break on >1.5× volume with A ≥ 3.
  • Entry veto: never enter while oil makes new spike highs or 10Y rises >10bps/day — the shock is live.
  • Exit: scale out on 2 froth signals (price >25% over 50d MA, extreme funding, alt mania); out on 3+.
  • Correlation breakdown (BTC↔Nasdaq < 0.2): driver is crypto-specific → weight ETF flows & sentiment over macro.
